Tools & Technologies

For effective website optimization and performance monitoring, I utilize a range of powerful tools. Google Analytics and Google Search Console are used to track site traffic and monitor search performance. Ahrefs and SEMrush help with keyword tracking and backlink analysis, providing insights for strategic SEO planning. Technical audits are conducted using Screaming Frog to ensure site health, while on-page optimization is managed with tools like Yoast SEO and RankMath. Additionally, PageSpeed Insights and Lighthouse are employed to evaluate and enhance website performance, ensuring fast and efficient user experiences.
